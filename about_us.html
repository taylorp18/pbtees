What we're about

P.B. Tee's is a business started for and by college students. As someone paying tens of thousands of dollars just to take classes and live in a dorm, we decided enough was enough with fashion costing hundreds more dollars on top of it all.

So, we make clothes that are well-made, sustainably sourced, and, of course, stylish. We don't believe in making the price jump to several times what it would cost at T.J. Maxx, just because of a logo. So we turn brands on their head, and sell them for a that price you (yes you) can actually afford.
